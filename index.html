Our Story

A True Stockton Tradition

More than just a restaurant — we're where the community gathers, stories are shared, and memories are made.

Welcome to the Heart of Stockton

Nestled right off Highway 20 in the charming town of Stockton, Illinois, Buckhorn Saloon has been a cornerstone of the local dining and nightlife scene for years. What started as a humble gathering place has grown into one of the most beloved American restaurants and bars in Galena Country.

Our mission is simple: serve incredible food, pour cold drinks, and treat every guest like family. Whether you're a local regular or a traveler passing through, you'll find a warm welcome, friendly service, and a relaxed atmosphere that keeps people coming back.

From our mouthwatering burgers and hearty sandwiches to our homemade pizzas and crowd-pleasing appetizers, every dish is crafted with care using quality ingredients. Pair it with a cold beer from our diverse selection, and you've got the perfect small-town dining experience.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here's everything you need to know.

Yes! We have ample parking available right in front of the restaurant on East North Avenue. There's also street parking available along the surrounding blocks. Parking is always free.

Reservations are recommended for Friday and Saturday evenings, as well as for special events like our Friday Fish Fry and Saturday Prime Rib. Walk-ins are always welcome, but a reservation guarantees your spot.

Absolutely! Buckhorn Saloon is a family-friendly restaurant. We welcome guests of all ages and have a kids' menu available. High chairs and booster seats are available upon request.

Well-behaved pets are welcome on our outdoor patio area. Please keep pets on a leash and be mindful of other guests. Service animals are always welcome inside.

Yes! We offer full takeaway service. Call us at +1 (815) 947-2352 to place your order, and we'll have it ready for pickup. Most menu items are available for takeaway.

We accept cash, Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover. We also accept Apple Pay and Google Pay for contactless payments.

Yes! We love hosting private events. Whether it's a birthday party, corporate gathering, or family reunion, we can accommodate groups of various sizes. Call us to discuss your event needs.

Yes! We have multiple TVs throughout the bar and dining area showing all major sports. NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL, college sports — we've got you covered. Ask your server to switch to your game.

Yes, Buckhorn Saloon is fully wheelchair accessible. We have a ramp at the entrance and accessible restrooms. Please let us know if you need any additional accommodations.

We're open Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday, and Sunday from 11:00 AM to 1:00 AM. We are closed on Wednesdays. Kitchen hours may vary slightly — call to confirm.

Yes! We offer several vegetarian options including our Veggie Burger, Margherita Pizza, and various salads. We also have gluten-free options available. Please inform your server of any dietary restrictions.

We regularly host live music, trivia nights, and special events. Follow us on Facebook for the latest schedule of upcoming events and entertainment.

Currently, we accept phone orders for takeaway at +1 (815) 947-2352. We're working on adding online ordering soon. Stay tuned!

Yes! Gift cards are available for purchase at the restaurant. They make a great gift for friends and family who love great food and cold drinks.

No dress code! Come as you are. Buckhorn Saloon is a casual, welcoming environment where everyone feels comfortable. Jeans, boots, and a smile are all you need.

Yes, we offer free Wi-Fi for all guests. Ask your server for the password when you arrive. Perfect for checking scores or sharing photos of your meal!

Our Buckhorn Burger is our #1 seller, followed closely by the Friday Fish Fry and Saturday Prime Rib. The Buffalo Wings and Loaded Cheese Fries are also fan favorites!

We're currently developing a loyalty program. In the meantime, follow us on Facebook for exclusive specials, discounts, and event announcements for our regulars.

Absolutely! We welcome you to bring your own birthday cake. Just let us know when you arrive or make your reservation, and we'll be happy to store it and serve it with candles.

Yes, we can accommodate large groups and offer catering for off-site events. Contact us at +1 (815) 947-2352 to discuss catering options and group pricing.
